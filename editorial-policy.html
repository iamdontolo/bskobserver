Editorial Policy

Our public commitments to accuracy, independence and accountability.

Independence

BSK Observer accepts no direction from any political party, government body, advertiser or donor. Editorial decisions rest with the Editor-in-Chief and the newsroom, guided solely by journalistic merit, factual accuracy, and the public interest.

Accuracy & Fact-Checking

Every reported claim is verified against at least one independent source. Contested claims require corroboration from two or more independent sources. Where we cannot verify, we say so plainly.

Corrections

When we get something wrong, we correct it — promptly, clearly, and without excuse. Corrections appear at the top of the affected article and, where material, are re-broadcast on BSK TV Network. Submit corrections to corrections@bskobserver.com.

Sources & Attribution

We prefer named sources. We use anonymous sources only when the information is of clear public interest and cannot be obtained on the record, and we explain the reason for anonymity in the story.

Opinion & Commentary

Opinion pieces are clearly labelled and represent the views of their authors, not of BSK Observer. Facts within opinion pieces are still fact-checked.

Ethics & Standards

Our journalists follow the professional standards of the Sierra Leone Association of Journalists (SLAJ) and comparable international codes. We do not pay for information. We do not accept gifts of material value. We disclose relevant conflicts of interest.

Reader Complaints

Complaints about our journalism should be sent to the Editor-in-Chief at eic@bskobserver.com. We commit to acknowledging every complaint within three business days.
